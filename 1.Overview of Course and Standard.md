### ISO Fact
#### ISO = International Organization for Standardization
1. ISO is based on the Greek word ISO, which means "equal" or standardization, as the universal short form of its name.
>  ISO基於希臘語，ISO意爲"等值"或標準化，作爲其名稱的普遍簡略形式。
2. ISO is an independent Non-Government Organization and its headquarter is in Geneva - Switzerland.
> ISO是一個獨立的非政府組織，其總部設在日內瓦-瑞士。
3. ISO is made up of more than 160 National Standards Bodies ("NSBs")
> ISO由160多個國家標準機構組成。
4. All standards are based on international consensus.
> 所有標準均以國際共識爲基礎。
6. ISO 27001 is a standard for Information Security Management System.
> ISO27001是資訊安全管理系統的標準。

ISO 27001 standard focuses on Information Security Management System (ISMS) principles, risk-based thinking, and relation of this standard with other management system standards.
> ISO27001標準着重於資訊安全管理系統原則、風險爲本思考及資訊安全的關係，本標準與其他管理體系標準一致。
#### ISO standards are usually developed by its Technical Committees ("TCs") and Sub-committees ("SCs").
> 國際標準化組織標準通常由其技術委員會("TCs")制定,以及委員會(下稱「委員會」)的職權範圍。
1. Technical Committee HS/1, Information Security Management, is responsible for development and modification of ISO 27001 standard for information security management system certification.
> 資訊安全管理技術委員會HS/1，負責ISO27001的開發和修改資訊安全管理系統認證的標準。
#### ISO 27001:2013 was published on 1s October 2013.
#### ISO 27001:2013 latest standard gives Message in 4 line
> ISO27001:2013最新標準4個信條
 
1. Plan what you do (Planning is important for any activity; you have to plan for resources / objectives / activit ies / controls / procedures, etc. for ISMS.)
> 1.計劃自己的工作（計劃對於任何活動都是很重要的，你必須計劃自己的工作）(ISMS的資源/目標/活動/控制/程序等)
2. Do what you say (Implement your plan; deploy resources and confirm with the plan.)
> 2.按你說的做(實施你的計劃，部署資源，並確認你的計劃)。
3. Check what you do (Monitor and measure your activities and results for conformity and effectiveness of system.)
> 3.檢查你的行爲(監察和衡量你的活動及結果是否符合規定)
及系統的成效。)
4. Act (Analyze / review / decide / change / improve the effectiveness)
> 4.行動(分析/檢討/決定/更改/改善成效)

#### Purpose of ISMS and Standard
1. The purpose of information security management system is to incorporate the Information Security Management Systems (ISMS) into the overall management system of the organization.
> 資訊安全管理系統的目的是把資訊安全管理系統納入機構的整體管理系統。
2. The title of the standard is:ISO 2700 1:2013 - information security management system requirements
3. The standard provides a framework to assist organisation of information security. It provides a set of requirements that organisations must achieve.
該標準提供一個架構協助組織資訊保安。它提供了組織必須達到的要求。

#### Benefits of ISO 2700 1:2013 Information Security Management System
A well-functioning ISMS provides confidence to the organization and its
various stakeholders, and gives the benefits as listed below:
> 運作良好的國際信息系統審計(ISMS)爲機構及其機構提供信心。並列出以下各項好處:
1. Enables organization to address information security in a practical, cost-effective, realistic and comprehensive manner;
> 1.使組織能夠在一個切實可行的、具成本效益的、切合實際的資訊安全問題中處理資訊安全問題。和全面的方式;
2. Establishes mutual trust between networked sites;
> 2.建立聯網網站之間的互信;
3. Enhances Quality Assurance - ISO 9000, SEI-CMMI;
> 3.加強品質保證-ISO9000、SEI-CMMI;
4. Demonstrates a high and appropriate standard of security;
> 4.顯示出高標準和適當的安全標準;
5. Increases the ability of organization to manage and survive a disaster;
> 5.提高組織管理和生存能力;
6. Gives better control on information security system, which means more satisfied customers leading to increased business prospects and better market image;
> 6.更好地控制信息安全系統，這意味著讓客戶更滿意可以帶來更大的業務前景和更好的市場形象；
7. Increases level of motivation, co-operation, workmanship and information security awareness.
> 提高動機，合作，工藝和資訊安全意識。

#### Certification Process
![](https://i.imgur.com/Wts7ZLn.png)
1. Preparation for documentation and implementation audit 
> 1.準備文件和實施審核
2. Helps organization to familiarize themselves with the certification audit approach
> 2.幫助組織熟悉認證審核方法

> 3.涵蓋所有ISO 27001要求

* Stage-1 audit is conducted to verify adequacy of documents and it is conducted at client site
> 進行第一階段審核以核實文件的充分性，並在客戶現場進行
* Stage-2 audit is a compliance audit and all departments are audited.
> 第二階段審核是合規審核，所有部門都經過審核。
* Details of stage 1 and stage2 audit is given in next slide
> 下面會詳細介紹了第一階段和第二階段審核

#### Stage-1 Audit
1. Recommended to be done on site;
> 1.建議在現場進行；
2. Audit of informat ion security management system documentation;
> 2.審核信息安全管理系統文件；
3. Evaluation of location / site specific conditions;
> 3.評估地點/地點的具體情況；
4. Review of key perfor mances parameters;
> 4.審查關鍵績效參數；
5. Validation of scope;
> 5.範圍的確認；
6. Collection of information regarding statutory & regulatory requirements and their compliance;
> 6.收集有關法律法規要求及其遵守情況的信息；
7. Review of al location of resources for stage-2 audit, agree with client and plan for Stage-2;
> 7.審查第二階段審核的所有資源位置，與客戶和第二階段的計劃達成一致；
8. Evaluation of Internal audits & Management reviews;
> 8.評估內部審核和管理評審;
9. Assess the overall readiness for Stage-2 audit;
> 9.評估第二階段審核的總體準備情況；
10. Report the findings including areas of concern to the Client/Auditee.
> 10.向客戶/學員報告調查結果，包括關注的領域。

#### Stage-2 Audit

##### The purpose :
Evaluation of implementation, including effectiveness of the system
(must be conducted on-site) below points are considered in stage-2
audit.
> 評估執行情況，包括系統的有效性(必須在現場進行）在第二階段審核中要考慮以下幾點。
1. Conformity to all requirements of the audit criteria.
> 1.符合審核標準的所有要求。
2. Performance against information security objectives.
> 2.針對資訊安全目標的性能。
3. Performance against legal and regulatory compliance.
> 3.違反法律和法規要求的表現。
4. Operational control of clients' processes.
> 4.客戶流程的操作控制。
5. Internal audit and management review results, actions and their effectiveness at locations.
> 5.內部審計和管理人員審查結果，行動及其在各地點的有效性。
6. Management's responsibility for their policies.
> 6.管理層對其政策的責任。
7. Linkages between Statutory & regulatory requirements, Information security policy, performance objectives and targets
> 法規與法規要求，資訊安全政策，績效目標
8. Evaluate system's effectiveness in:
    * Achieving objectives & targets
    > 實現目標
    * Implementing policy commitments (i.e. compliance, meeting requirements, continual improvement, etc)
    > 履行政策承諾（即合規性，滿足要求，持續改進等）
    * Operational controls in all areas of Information security management system
    > 資訊安全管理系統各個領域的操作控制
    * Corrective actions
    > 糾正措施
9. Evaluate the overall implementation and effectiveness of the organisation's information security management system
> 9.評估組織信息的整體實施和有效性安全管理系統
10. Its full system audit.
> 10.其完整的系統審核。
#### Audit Conclusion
1. Audit conclusion is made on the stage-1 and stage-2 audit results.
> 1.根據第一階段和第二階段的審計結果得出審計結論。
2. The auditor can do the recommendation of the audit results.
> 2.審核員可以對審核結果提出建議。
3. The final certification decision is taken by the technical review committee.
> 3.最終的認證決定由技術審核委員會決定。
4. Based on the successful review by committee the certificate is issued or otherwise.
> 4.基於委員會的成功審核，證書將以其他方式頒發。
5. In case of decision to award the certificate the surveillance audits are carried out based on the frequency decided.
> 5.如果決定授予證書，則根據確定的頻率進行監督審核。
#### Surveillance Audits
> 監視審核
1. Conducted at least once per year at on-site;
> 1.每年至少進行一次現場檢查；
2. Covers all functions / processes over a three year period subsequent to certification/ Re-certification audits;
> 2.涵蓋認證/重新認證後三年內的所有功能/流程
3. Audit plan based upon results of previous audits & importance & status of processes;
> 3.根據先前審核的結果，過程的重要性和狀態來製定審核計劃；
4. Internal audits may be taken into account during surveill ance audit planning;
> 4.在監視審計計劃中可以考慮內部審計；
5. Assess organisations' continued conformance to the certified standard requirements.
> 5.評估組織是否繼續符合認證標準要求。
#### Re-certification visits
1. These are required every 3 years whereby the whole system is subjected to a full assessment usually in one visit including documentation review and the implementation audit.
> 1.這些要求每3年進行一次，以便通常一次訪問就對整個系統進行全面評估，包括文檔審查和審核。實施審核。
2. Certificates are therefore valid for 3 years only.
> 2.證書的有效期僅為3年。
3. Surveill ance audits are required every year to monitor the certification.
> 3.每年需要進行監視審核以監視認證。
